BLUEPRINTS

Each building is generated with 3 layouts that you can build if you have the right blueprints. Some layouts produce points and can be essential to climbing league rankings!

When you view a building before buying it from the map, only 2 of the 3 layouts are visible. You need to buy this building to know the nature of it's 3rd layout.

Blueprints can be acquired by combining 10x ideas, packages, end-of season league rewards, or purchased from the AMAZOPOLIS in-game shop.
