EMPLOYEES

Employees can be assigned to buildings and grant you different bonuses. At the end of each season, 25% of your employees will be promoted to the next level and granted greater bonuses. You can recruit an employee by collecting 10 resumes, or purchase them from the AMAZOPOLIS in-game shop.

SUPER EMPLOYEES were introduced in the September 2022 season and grant you more powerful bonuses than regular employees. You have a 10% chance of earning a SUPER EMPLOYEE when discovering a random employee. Super Employees may also be purchased from the AMAZOPOLIS in-game shop. Super Employees DO NOT level up.

Both Employees and Super Employees are kept from one season to the next!
